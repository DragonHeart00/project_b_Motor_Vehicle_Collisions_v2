Crimes in San Francisco- Analyzing Changes in Crime Patterns over Time

Updated 12:34 PM CEST, Fri May 12, 2023

Introduction

San Francisco is known for its picturesque location, culture, and diverse communities. However, it has also been known for its crime rates. The San Francisco Police Department keeps detailed records of all reported crimes, including the type of crime, the date and time it occurred, and its location. The San Francisco crime dataset contains information about incidents reported to the San Francisco Police Department (SFPD) from 2003 to 2018. The dataset is publicly available and is updated on a daily basis. It includes detailed information about the time, location,category, and description of each incident. The dataset contains over 1.5 million incidents and is used by law enforcement agencies, researchers, journalists, and concerned citizens to analyze crime trends and inform decision-making.
